How The System Drives Value

⚡

Frictionless Experience

Paper feedback forms are dead. Customers simply scan a beautiful QR code at the billing counter. The form opens instantly, pre-configured for that specific branch. A 5-second tap-and-submit process ensures maximum participation rates.

📍

GPS-Verified Integrity

Data accuracy is paramount. When a customer submits feedback, the system silently captures their exact GPS coordinates. This ensures complete transparency and prevents fraudulent reviews from being submitted off-premises.

🤖

Zero-Touch Automation

Management shouldn't have to hunt for data. The system features an automated background engine that compiles branch performance metrics and delivers a stunning HTML report directly to your email inbox (Daily, Weekly, or Monthly).

🎛️

Centralized Command

A highly secure, beautiful Admin Dashboard provides a real-time birds-eye view of all operations. Track branch satisfaction ratings, filter by date, analyze complaint categories, and generate new QR codes with a single click.

💎

The Zero-Cost Architecture

The most impressive technical achievement of this software is its infrastructure. By leveraging Google's Serverless Cloud (Apps Script) and GitHub's Global CDN, we have eliminated the need for rented servers.


The Result: You own a state-of-the-art Enterprise Software worth thousands of dollars in annual licensing fees—running at an ongoing operational cost of $0 per month.
