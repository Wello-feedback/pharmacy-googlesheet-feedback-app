Smart Feedback System

A Complete Overview of Wello Pharmacy's New Digital Solution

Aapne ek modern, paperless aur smart customer feedback system ki demand ki thi, taaki sabhi branches ki performance par nazar rakhi ja sake. Yeh software usi vision ko hakikat me badalta hai. Yahan samajhiye ye kaise kaam karta hai aur isme kya naye features hain:

Core Business Features

📱

Easy Customer Experience

Customers ko lambe form nahi bharne padenge. Har branch ke counter par ek QR Code hoga. Scan karte hi ek sundar mobile form khulega, jahan branch ka naam apne aap aa jayega. Bas Stars dabao aur submit karo!

📧

Automated Email Reports

Aapko roz dashboard kholne ki zaroorat nahi! System automatically aapke inbox me reports bhejega (Daily, Weekly, ya Monthly). Isme bataya jayega ki kis branch ko kitne stars mile aur kin cheezon me sudhaar (improvement) chahiye.

📍

GPS Location Verification

Fake reviews se bachne ke liye, jab customer form submit karta hai toh system unki GPS location (Latitude/Longitude) record kar leta hai. Isse aap verify kar sakte hain ki review sach me pharmacy ke andar se diya gaya hai ya nahi.

📊

Centralized Admin Dashboard

Ek secure login ke sath ek Dashboard banaya gaya hai. Yahan aapko sabhi branches ka poora control milta hai. Kis branch ki performance kharab chal rahi hai, customer kya shikayat kar rahe hain, ye sab live ek jagah dikhta hai.

Yeh System Kaam Kaise Karta Hai?

1

Customer Store me Aata Hai

Dawai lene ke baad, billing counter par customer QR code scan karta hai. System turant pehchan leta hai ki ye QR code kis branch ka hai (e.g. Civil Lines ya Burari).

2

Rating & Comments

Customer apni rating (1 se 5 star) deta hai. Agar rating kam hoti hai, toh system aapse reason puchta hai (jaise: Medicines not available, Staff behavior rude). Sath me apna naam aur number daal kar Submit karta hai.

3

Live Data Update

Jaise hi customer form bharta hai, data instantly background me aapki Google Sheet aur Admin Dashboard me automatically save ho jata hai. Koi paper ka jhanjhat nahi, sab digital.

4

Smart Owner Notification

Agar aapne Weekly email set kiya hai, toh har hafte system saara data jod (calculate) karke ek professional HTML design wala Email aapke phone/laptop par bhej dega. Jisme total feedback, average rating, aur sabse badi shikayatein (tags) highlight hongi.

💡

Zero-Cost, Zero-Maintenance Setup

Is software ki sabse badi khasiyat ye hai ki isme koi Monthly Server Cost (hosting fees) nahi lagegi. Ise poori tarah Google aur GitHub ki free enterprise technologies ka use karke banaya gaya hai, jo kabhi down nahi hota aur bohot fast chalta hai.
